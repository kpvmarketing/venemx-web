Our Story

Where Two Cultures Share the Table

Venemex Bakery & Café was born from a simple, powerful idea: Venezuela and Mexico share a passion for bold flavors, warm hospitality, and food made from the heart. We bring both traditions together under one roof, right here in Daytona Beach.

From our freshly brewed coffee to golden tequeños, hearty empanadas, Mexican tortas, and handcrafted pastries — every bite is a journey across two countries.
