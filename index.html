Our Story

Where Two Cultures Share the Table

Venemex Bakery & Café was born from a simple, powerful idea: Venezuela and Mexico share a passion for bold flavors, warm hospitality, and food made from the heart. We are a family restaurant — and we bring both traditions together under one roof, right here in Daytona Beach.

From our freshly brewed coffee to golden tequeños, hearty empanadas, Mexican tortas, and handcrafted pastries — every bite is a journey across two countries, and every visit feels like coming home.
